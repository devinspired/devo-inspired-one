ABOUT US

We are a human resources management consulting firm with broad experience in staffing in government and the private sector. We are located in Ottawa, Canada.

We believe in educating candidates so that everyone has an equal chance of being selected according to their qualifications. It promotes diversity and contributes to the quality of life for many. With our experience in designing and coordinating staffing processes, we can give you the inside track on the requirements to be screened in for an interview, pass an exam or have a successful interview.
