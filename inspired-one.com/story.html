OUR STORY


Inspired ONE was founded in 2008 by Bryan Gauer and Devin Brown. The former teammates came together to produce their first documentary film for the McMaster Marauder football team. Based on the success of the film, Inspired ONE was incorporated and began producing a variety of media and film content for a wide range of clients.

Productions include Video Tours, Promotional Videos, Music Videos, Documentaries, Event Highlights, Behind the Scenes, Corporate Video, Web and TV Commercials, Television Productions, and Short Films.

From the beginning Inspired ONE has always been about working with talented and creative minds to produce work that is entertaining, motivating, and inspiring.
