What We Do

We tell stories

Through the use of digital media, we produce video, film, and television content from the ground up. We have ideas for every client about how to share their stories and create brand culture through the use of video marketing and advertising. We see the potential in everything. We work with a network of filmmakers who strive to push creative boundaries while effectively telling compelling stories.

If you have message or anything to sell your best tool is video content; whether you're an artist, musician, a small business or a large corporation. With smart phones capable of HD video, and access to content and stories from all over the planet. People are bombarded constantly with video content, Inspired will help you separate your content from the rest and tell your story.

We strongly believe the power of film is the absolute best way to spread a message and create change regardless of what you are saying. Short, impactful, memorable commercials could be the key to success for your business. Explore the possibilities with us, lets get creative and help your brand stand out.

Everyone has a story, and we want to help you share it.

Who We Are

Inspired ONE is a film production company consisting of a team of filmmakers and multimedia artists who strive to push creative limits with each new production.

Founded in 2008 by Bryan Gauer and Devin Brown during the production of their first documentary film about the McMaster Marauder’s football team’ season. Based on the response and success of the film, Inspired ONE was incorporated and began producing a variety of media and film content through various departments within McMaster, and for a wide range of clients.

Previous productions include Video Tours, Promotional Videos, Music Videos, Documentaries, Event Highlights, Behind the Scenes, Corporate Video, Web and TV Commercials, TV Productions, and Short Films. From the beginning Inspired ONE has always been about working with talented, driven team members to produce work that is entertaining, motivating, and inspiring.
